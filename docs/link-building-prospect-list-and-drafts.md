### Include Prospects When

- they already link out to third-party resources
- they publish AI tool roundups, workflow roundups, learning resources, or niche prompt content
- their audience overlaps with one of the five priority pages
- the page you want updated is still active or has been refreshed recently
- the site is small-to-mid sized but relevant enough that a real link is plausible

### Skip Prospects When

- the page is clearly abandoned or years out of date
- they never link out to external resources
- the site is generic SEO sludge with no editorial judgment
- the audience is too broad and the fit is weak
- the only contact path is obviously low-intent and spammy

### Priority Order

Start with these prospect buckets:

1. AI newsletters with curated links
2. prompt engineering education posts and resource pages
3. AI workflow and productivity blogs
4. AI art / Stable Diffusion resource pages
5. founder and operator tool-stack roundups

### Personalization Rule

Every outreach message needs one real hook:

- a recent post
- a specific resource page
- a recurring roundup section
- a stated audience problem your page actually solves

If you cannot write a real hook in one sentence, the prospect is not ready yet.

### Page Matching Rules

- Pitch the homepage when the prospect maintains broad AI resources, tools, or workflow lists.
- Pitch `Prompt Engineering 101` when the prospect teaches AI basics, onboarding, or learning workflows.
- Pitch `Best ChatGPT Prompts for 2026` when the prospect curates ChatGPT or general AI productivity resources.
- Pitch `Best Stable Diffusion Prompts` when the prospect covers image generation, AI art, or creative tooling.
- Pitch `Prompt Templates` when the prospect talks about systems, repeatable workflows, productivity, or prompt operations.
